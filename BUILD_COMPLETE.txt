To Add New Furniture:
  1. Create sprite: /public/sprites/furnitures/{id}.png
  2. Add to FURNITURE_REGISTRY in lib/furniture/FurnitureRegistry.ts
  3. Add marketplace item to data/marketplace.ts

To Add New Clothing:
  1. Add to CLOTHING_ITEMS in data/clothing.ts
  2. Create sprite: /public/sprites/avatar/{category}/{id}.png
